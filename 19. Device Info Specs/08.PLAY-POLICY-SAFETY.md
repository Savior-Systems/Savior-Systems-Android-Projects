# 08. Play Store Policy & Data Safety

This document outlines Google Play privacy policies for **Device Info Specs**.

---

## 1. Requested Permissions
To maximize user trust and prevent automated app flags during review, the app requests **zero permissions**.

No location, contact, phone, storage, camera, or network permissions are declared.

---

## 2. Google Play Data Safety Declarations

### A. Data Collection
*   **Data Collected**: None.
*   The application does not collect or transmit any user data (e.g., CPU speeds, RAM sizes, sensor values) to external servers.

### B. Data Sharing
*   **Data Shared**: None.
*   No personal data, device identifiers, or hardware specs are shared with third-party networks or service providers.

### C. Data Security Measures
*   **Encryption**: No data leaves the device.
*   **Data Deletion**: Since no offline data is stored, no deletion actions are required.
