# 12. Logging & Analytics
`
This document details logging rules and analytics setup for **Device Info Specs**.
`
---
`
## 1. Privacy Mandate
*   **Zero PII**: Unique hardware serial numbers, device names, or IMEI values must **never** be logged to analytics dashboards.
`
---
`
## 2. Analytics Events
`
### Event: Diagnostics Executed (`diagnostic_run`)
Fired when a user executes a benchmark test.
*   `cores_count`: `INTEGER` (Number of logical CPUs)
*   `android_version`: `INTEGER` (OS level API)
