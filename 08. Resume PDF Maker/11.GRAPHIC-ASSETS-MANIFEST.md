# 11. Graphic Asset Manifest

## 1. Required Assets
1. **App Icon (`ic_launcher`)**
   - 512x512 PNG. A sleek briefcase or a document with a tie. Color: Navy Blue & White.
2. **Empty State Illustrations**
   - `illust_empty_dashboard.xml`: A vector art of a person holding a blank paper.
3. **Template Thumbnails**
   - Small preview images (WebP format) of what the templates look like (e.g., `thumb_harvard.webp`) to show in the Template Selection screen.
4. **Icons**
   - Standard Material icons for Edit, Delete, Add (+), Export (FileDownload).
