# 06. Ad Monetization Map

## 1. Ad Strategy
- **Primary:** Google AdMob.
- **Format:** Banners, Interstitials, and Rewarded Video.

## 2. Placements
1. **Banner Ad:**
   - **Location:** Anchored to the bottom of the `DashboardScreen` and `EditorHubScreen`.
   - **Strict Rule:** Do NOT place banners on the data-entry form screens. Users need their full keyboard and screen real estate to type resumes.
   
2. **Interstitial Ad:**
   - **Trigger:** Show after the user taps "Export PDF" AND the PDF successfully generates. 
   - **Frequency:** Use a 180-second cooldown timer. Do not spam them if they export 5 times in a row adjusting margins.

3. **Rewarded Video Ad (The Scale Up):**
   - **Trigger:** The "Harvard" and "Creative" templates are locked. The user taps them -> Dialog: "Watch a short video to unlock this premium template for this resume."
   - **Result:** Upon completion, update `ResumeEntity.isPremiumUnlocked = true` in the DB.
