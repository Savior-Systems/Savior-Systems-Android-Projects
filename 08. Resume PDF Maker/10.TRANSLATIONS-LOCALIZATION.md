# 10. Translation & Localization

## 1. Scope
MVP will be English-only. However, resumes have highly standardized labels.

## 2. Strings Map
Rookie Developer MUST use `strings.xml` for all form labels to allow future localization into Spanish/French.

```xml
<resources>
    <!-- Section Titles -->
    <string name="section_personal">Personal Details</string>
    <string name="section_education">Education</string>
    <string name="section_experience">Work Experience</string>
    <string name="section_skills">Skills &amp; Languages</string>
    
    <!-- Form Hints -->
    <string name="hint_company_name">Company Name</string>
    <string name="hint_degree">Degree or Certification</string>
    
    <!-- Actions -->
    <string name="action_export_pdf">Export to PDF</string>
</resources>
```
