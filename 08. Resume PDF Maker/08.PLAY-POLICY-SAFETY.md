# 08. Play Policy & Safety

## 1. Storage Permissions (Crucial Risk)
- **Do NOT ask for `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` on Android 11+ (API 30+).** Google Play will reject the app.
- **Solution:** Use the `MediaStore` API or `Intent.ACTION_CREATE_DOCUMENT` (Storage Access Framework) to let the user pick where to save the PDF.

## 2. User Data Privacy
- A Resume contains highly sensitive PII (Personally Identifiable Information) like Phone numbers, Addresses, and Emails.
- **Policy:** The Privacy Policy MUST explicitly state that all data is stored entirely via SQLite locally on the device, and no PII is transmitted to any servers. 

## 3. Ad Policy
- Ensure Rewarded Ads clearly state "Watch Ad to Unlock Template". Do not deceive users into clicking ads.
