# 07. ASO & Play Store Listing Strategy

## 1. App Store Metadata

### English (US / Global)
* **App Title:** Resume Builder App: PDF Maker
* **Short Description:** Create professional, ATS-friendly resumes and CVs in minutes. Export offline.
* **Long Description:**
Land your dream job with **Resume Builder App: PDF Maker**! Build professional, ATS-compliant resumes and curriculum vitae (CV) in minutes, entirely offline.

Whether you are a fresh graduate looking for your first job, a freelancer, or an experienced corporate professional, our offline CV maker app offers structured layouts to help you stand out.

**Why Choose Resume Builder App?**
* **100% Offline Privacy:** Your personal information never leaves your device. No cloud storage, no data collection, no account registrations required.
* **Selectable Vector PDFs:** Our PDF generator compiles selectable, search-friendly vector text (never rasterized images). This ensures 100% ATS compliance and allows you to edit the output PDF directly in other productivity apps like Canva or Adobe Acrobat.
* **Template Customization:** Select from Classic Navy, Emerald Corporate, or Onyx Modern palettes. Adjust font pairings (Modern Sans, Classic Serif, Tech Mono) and page margins (Compact, Normal, Wide) to fit your content.
* **Backup & Restore:** Save your raw CV profiles to local JSON backup files. Share backups between devices easily.
* **Direct Sharing & Printing:** Open your PDF instantly with a single tap in Canva, Google Drive, or send it directly via email/Slack.
