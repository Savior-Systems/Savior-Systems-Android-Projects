# 07. ASO & Play Store Listing Strategy

## 1. Metadata
**App Title:** Resume Builder App: PDF Maker
**Short Description:** Create professional, ATS-friendly resumes and CVs in minutes. Export to PDF.
**Long Description:**
Land your dream job with Resume Builder App! Whether you are a fresh graduate or an experienced professional, our app helps you generate ATS-compliant, stunning PDF resumes entirely offline.

Features:
- Multiple Professional Templates (Harvard, Minimalist, Modern).
- Easy step-by-step data entry.
- Manage multiple CVs for different job applications.
- 100% Offline & Secure. Your data never leaves your phone.
- 1-Tap PDF Export.

## 2. Keyword Targeting
- Primary: resume builder free, cv maker app, ats resume formatting, pdf resume creator, make curriculum vitae.

## 3. Screenshots Strategy
1. **Showcase Templates:** Split screen showing 3 different professional PDF outputs.
2. **Dashboard:** Show the management of multiple profiles.
3. **Forms:** Show the clean, distraction-free UI.
4. **Offline Tag:** A badge explicitly stating "100% Offline - Absolute Privacy".
