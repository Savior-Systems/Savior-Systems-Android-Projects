# 08. Play Store Policy & Safety — Expense Diary Local
`
This document details Savior Systems compliance rules, minimised permissions, Google Play Data Safety requirements, and the 2025/2026 Financial Features Declaration for Expense Diary Local.
`
---
`
## 1. App Permission Scopes
`
To prevent Google Play policy warnings and maximize conversion rates, Expense Diary Local runs with the **absolute minimum permissions** required.
`
| Permission | Required By | Runtime? | Justification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `android.permission.INTERNET` | AdMob SDK, Firebase Analytics | No | Ad loading and anonymous telemetry. |
| `android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE` | AdMob SDK | No | Network availability check before ad requests. |
| `android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Budget Alert System | **Yes** (API 33+) | Local budget threshold push notifications. |
| `android.permission.USE_BIOMETRIC` | App Lock Feature | No | Fingerprint/Face authentication gate. |
| `android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` | CSV/PDF Export | **Yes** (API < 29 only) | Saving exported files. Scoped storage on API 29+. |
`
### 1.1 Permissions NOT Requested
*   ❌ `READ_CONTACTS` — No social features.
*   ❌ `CAMERA` — No receipt scanning in V1.
*   ❌ `LOCATION` — Not needed; AdMob uses approximate location from IP.
*   ❌ `READ_SMS` — No auto-categorization from bank SMS.
*   ❌ `PHONE_STATE` — No device ID tracking.

## 2. Google Play Data Safety Declarations
`
### 2.1 Data Collection Mapping
`
| Data Type | Collected? | Shared? | Purpose | Optional? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Financial Info (Expenses)** | ✅ Stored locally only | ❌ Never shared | Core app functionality | No |
| **App Activity** | ✅ | ❌ | Firebase Analytics (anonymous) | No |
| **App Performance** | ✅ | ❌ | Crashlytics crash reporting | No |
| **Device Identifiers** | ✅ | ✅ AdMob | Advertising personalization | No |
| **Approximate Location** | ✅ by AdMob | ✅ AdMob | Localized ad delivery | No |
| **Personal Info (Name, Email)** | ❌ | ❌ | Not collected | — |
| **Photos/Videos** | ❌ | ❌ | Not collected | — |
| **Messages/Contacts** | ❌ | ❌ | Not collected | — |
`
### 2.2 Security Declarations
*   **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted from the app to Firebase/AdMob is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.3).
*   **Encryption at Rest:** The local Room database is encrypted using SQLCipher (AES-256-CBC) with keys managed by Android Keystore.
*   **Data Deletion Mechanism:** Users can trigger "Clear All Data" in Settings to delete all local database entities, preferences, and exported files instantly.
*   **No Account Required:** The app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.
`

## 3. Financial Features Declaration (2025/2026)
`
Google Play now requires all apps to complete the **Financial Features Declaration**, even if they do not offer financial services.
`
### 3.1 Declaration Responses
| Question | Answer |
| :--- | :--- |
| Does your app provide financial products or services? | **No** |
| Does your app facilitate lending, insurance, or trading? | **No** |
| Does your app connect to bank accounts? | **No** |
| Does your app process payments? | **No** |
| Does your app manage cryptocurrency? | **No** |
`
### 3.2 Classification Note
Expense Diary Local is a **personal utility tool** for self-tracking expenses. It does not:
*   Provide financial advice or recommendations.
*   Connect to any financial institution.
*   Process, store, or transmit payment credentials.
*   Offer credit, loans, or insurance services.
`
This classification positions the app as a **general utility** under Google Play's Finance category, exempting it from enhanced financial services documentation.

## 5. Privacy Policy Requirements
`
### 5.1 Mandatory Disclosures
*   **Data Storage**: All financial data is stored exclusively on the user's device.
*   **Third-Party SDKs**: Google AdMob (advertising), Firebase Analytics (anonymous telemetry), Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting).
*   **Data Sharing**: No user financial data is shared with any third party. Ad SDKs may collect device identifiers for ad personalization per Google's policies.
*   **Contact**: privacy@saviorsystems.com
`
### 5.2 Privacy Policy URL
*   Must be a publicly accessible HTTPS URL hosted on the Savior Systems website or GitHub Pages.
*   Must be linked in: Play Console → Store Listing → Privacy Policy field.
*   Must also be linked in: App → Settings → "Privacy Policy" menu item (in-app WebView or external browser intent).
