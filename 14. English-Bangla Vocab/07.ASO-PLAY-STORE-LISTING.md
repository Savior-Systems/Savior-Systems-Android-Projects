### Long Description (Max 4000 chars)
```
Master your English vocabulary with English to Bangla Vocabulary. A complete, offline-first educational app designed to help you search, study, and retain words.

Featuring a preloaded database of common words, meanings, pronunciations, and synonyms—no internet connection is ever required to look up a word.

CORE FEATURES:
• Offline Dictionary: Instant English-Bangla search results.
• Interactive Flashcards: Swipeable study cards to practice and commit words to memory.
• Vocabulary Quizzes: Put your spelling and definitions to the test with multiple-choice quizzes.
• Starred Word Decks: Bookmark difficult words to build personalized study lists.
• Offline Audios: Listen to natural English text-to-speech pronunciation readouts.

Perfect for HSC varsity admissions preparation and general language learning. Start memorizing vocabulary offline today!
```
