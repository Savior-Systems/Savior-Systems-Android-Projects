# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)
`
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: English-Bangla Vocab
*   **Category**: Education
*   **Core Objective**: Build a fast, offline dictionary and vocabulary-building app. It targets search intent for: `bangla dictionary`, `english to bangla`, and `vocab flashcards`.
*   **Value Proposition**: Pre-populated SQLite database, fast lookup speeds, offline search, and interactive daily flashcards.
`
---
`
## 2. Target Persona
### Persona: Ayan (HSC Candidate in Bangladesh)
*   **Needs**: Fast translation lookups and flashcard tests for preparation.
*   **Pain Points**: Standard vocabulary apps require an active internet connection to load translations, showing heavy ads.
*   **How the App Solves it**: The application uses a local pre-populated Room database that works offline, offering search results instantly.
`
---
`
## 3. Core Feature List
1.  **Instant Offline Search**:
    *   Search bar for quick English or Bengali keyword lookups.
    *   Pre-loaded database with over 20,000 common words.
2.  **Daily Flashcards**:
    *   Swipeable memory card deck displaying words, meanings, synonyms, and parts of speech.
3.  **Starred/Favorite Vocab**:
    *   One-tap bookmarking for personalized study decks.
4.  **Quiz Challenge**:
    *   Multiple-choice quiz (4 options) to test vocabulary memorization.
`
---
`
## 4. Monetization & Ad Zones
*   **Zero-Ad Zones**:
    *   Word definition lookups and flashcard swipe interactions are strictly zero-ad to prevent distraction.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   Anchored adaptive banner at the bottom of the search layout.
    *   Interstitial ad triggered upon completing a 10-question quiz, guarded by a **180s cooldown**.
`
