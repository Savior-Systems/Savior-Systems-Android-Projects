# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD) — BD Tax & VAT Calc
`
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: BD Tax & VAT Calc
*   **Category**: Finance / Utilities
*   **Core Objective**: Build a performant, 100% offline-first application that calculates individual income tax liability based on the latest Bangladesh progressive slab rates, coordinates VAT inclusion/exclusion, and stores records locally in an encrypted Room database.
*   **Target Market**: Bangladeshi salary earners, freelancers, and small business owners looking to compute taxes and VAT inputs quickly and securely.
*   **July 2026 Budget Readiness**: Slabs must be dynamically loaded from local asset configs so that the upcoming July budget update can be integrated with zero code changes.
`
---
`
## 2. Target Personas
`
### Persona A: Sajjad Hossain (34, Dhaka, Bangladesh)
*   **Role**: Senior Software Engineer (Salaried).
*   **Pain Point**: Needs to calculate his annual taxable income and investment rebate values to file tax returns but finds NBR forms and online calculators overly complex, slow, and full of advertisements.
*   **Usage**: Uses the Income Tax tab to select his exemption category (General, Woman/Senior, Disabled), inputs his monthly salary components, and gets a detailed tax liability slab-by-slab breakdown.
`
### Persona B: Tasnim Ara (42, Chittagong, Bangladesh)
*   **Role**: Boutique Shop Owner.
*   **Pain Point**: Sells clothes retail and needs to compute inclusive/exclusive VAT amounts at standard 15% and reduced 5% or 7.5% rates during invoicing without manual calculator mistakes.
*   **Usage**: Operates the VAT tab on her tablet, using presets to compute base price, VAT amount, and total bill instantly.
`
---
`
## 3. Core Feature List & User Stories
`
### Feature 1: Dynamic Progressive Income Tax Calculator
*   **Requirement**: Choose Tax Year (FY 2025–2026 default, extensible for FY 2026–2027 July Budget via JSON). Input baseline exemptions, salary segments, and housing/medical allowance. Output a progressive slab breakdown showing exactly how much tax is charged at 0%, 10%, 15%, 20%, 25%, and 30%.
*   **User Story**: *As Sajjad, I want to select my exemption tier and see how my salary fits into each tax slab so that I understand exactly where my tax is coming from.*
`
### Feature 2: VAT Inclusive/Exclusive Calculator
*   **Requirement**: A fast VAT toggle (Inclusive vs Exclusive) supporting standard 15% VAT and reduced presets (1.5%, 2%, 2.4%, 4.5%, 5%, 7.5%, 10%).
*   **User Story**: *As Tasnim, I want to calculate 7.5% VAT inclusive pricing on custom items so that my receipts are correctly compiled for NBR audits.*
`
### Feature 3: Calculations Registry & History Log
*   **Requirement**: Save calculations with custom titles (e.g. "Tax Return FY26", "Invoice 1042") locally. Support sorting by date and single-tap delete.
*   **User Story**: *As Sajjad, I want to save different salary configurations to compare my tax rebates across months.*
`
---
`
## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
*   **Cold Start Time**: < 400ms.
*   **Calculation Engine Lag**: Math execution must complete in < 5ms.
*   **Local Privacy**: 100% offline. Zero financial data is ever collected or sent off the device.
