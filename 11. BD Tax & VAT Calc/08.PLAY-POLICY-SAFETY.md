# 08. Play Policy & Data Safety — BD Tax & VAT Calc

Ensuring compliance with Play Store Financial App and user data privacy policies.

---

## 1. System Permissions Audit

### Required Permissions
*   No runtime permissions are requested. The app runs completely within the sandboxed user environment.

### Stripped / Unused Permissions (🚫 Zero Tolerance)
*   🚫 `android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` (Zero tracking)
*   🚫 `android.permission.READ_CONTACTS` (Not used)
*   🚫 `android.permission.INTERNET` (Only loaded by AdMob and Firebase Analytics SDKs; core math engines run locally)

---

## 2. Play Console Data Safety Questionnaire

All calculation entries, allowances, and history logs are stored locally in the Room SQLite container.

| Section Question | Answer Status | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Does your app collect or share user data?** | **No** | Calculations and histories exist strictly on-device. No data collection occurs. |
| **Is all user data encrypted in transit?** | **N/A** | No data leaves the device. |
| **Can users delete their history?** | **Yes** | Users can clear calculations list or reset database in Settings. |

---

## 3. Financial Services Declaration
*   **Policy Category**: Personal Financial Management.
*   **Safety Status**: The app is classified as a calculator/estimator. It does not provide financial credits, loans, or payment processing, avoiding heavy banking validation checks.
