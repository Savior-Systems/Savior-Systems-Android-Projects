# 06. AdMob Monetization Map

## Strategy
High-yield, low-friction monetization strategy designed not to violate Google Play "Disruptive Ads" policies.

## Ad Placements
1. **App Open Ad**: Displayed on cold boot (filtered via `consentManager`).
2. **Banner Ad / Native Advanced**: Anchored at the bottom of the Home screen or blended in lists.
3. **Interstitial Ad**: Fired after completing a major action (e.g., saving an item). 
   - **Cooldown**: STRICT 180-second cooldown timer between interstitial impressions.

## IAP (In-App Purchases)
- **Pro Tier**: $2.99 one-time purchase to remove all ads.
