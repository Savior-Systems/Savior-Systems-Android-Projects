# 08. Play Policy & Safety

## 1. Content Policy Compliance
- **No UGC (User Generated Content) for MVP:** Since all captions are hardcoded and pre-populated by us, we do not need a moderation system or a "Report User" button.
- **Family Policy:** Ensure all pre-populated captions are PG-13. DO NOT include hate speech, profanity, or sexually explicit content in the database.
- **Data Privacy:** The app stores everything offline in SQLite. We do not collect user data.
- **Privacy Policy URL:** A basic privacy policy must be generated (using a free generator) stating that the app uses AdMob (which collects advertising IDs) but otherwise collects no personal user data.

## 2. Ad Policy
- Do not place banner ads too close to buttons (like the Copy or Share button) to avoid accidental clicks, which violates AdMob policy.
- Provide a clear exit "X" button on Interstitials (handled by AdMob automatically, just don't obscure it).
