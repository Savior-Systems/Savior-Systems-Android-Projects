# 10. Translation & Localization

## 1. Scope for MVP
For the MVP, the app will be **English-only**.

## 2. Preparation for Future (Rookie Developer Instructions)
Even though it is English only, the rookie developer MUST NOT hardcode strings in the UI.
- **Rule:** Every UI text (e.g., "Favorites", "Categories", "No captions found", "Copied to clipboard") must be placed inside `res/values/strings.xml`.
- Do not put the actual database captions in `strings.xml`. Those belong in the Room Database prepopulation script.

### Example `strings.xml`:
```xml
<resources>
    <string name="app_name">Bio Status Captions</string>
    <string name="title_home">Categories</string>
    <string name="title_favorites">My Favorites</string>
    <string name="toast_copied">Copied to clipboard!</string>
    <string name="empty_favorites">No favorites yet. Tap the heart to save some!</string>
</resources>
```
