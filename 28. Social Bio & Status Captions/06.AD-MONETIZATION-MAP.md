# 06. Ad Monetization Map

## 1. Ad Network Strategy
- **Primary Network:** Google AdMob
- **Backup Strategy:** N/A for MVP.

## 2. Ad Placement Guidelines (Crucial for User Experience)
We do NOT want to spam the user. Too many ads lead to uninstalls.
Rookie Developer: Follow these placements exactly.

1. **Banner Ad (Anchored Bottom):**
   - **Location:** At the very bottom of the `HomeScreen` and `FavoritesScreen`.
   - **Do NOT place:** On the `CaptionListScreen` because it takes up valuable reading space and can cause accidental clicks when copying.
   
2. **Interstitial Ad (Full Screen):**
   - **Trigger:** Show an interstitial ad when the user navigates from the `CaptionListScreen` back to the `HomeScreen`.
   - **Frequency Capping:** Only show the interstitial ad once every 3 navigation actions (use a counter in ViewModel or SharedPreferences). DO NOT show it every single time they press back.

3. **Native Ad (Optional for MVP):**
   - Can be integrated into the list of captions (e.g., every 10th item is an ad). Only attempt this if Banner and Interstitial are working perfectly.
