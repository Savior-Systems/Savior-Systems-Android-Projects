# 12. Logging & Analytics

This document details logging rules and analytics setup for **WiFi QR Sharer**.

---

## 1. Privacy Mandate
*   **Zero PII**: SSID names and passwords scanned or generated must **never** be logged to analytics dashboards.

---

## 2. Analytics Events

### Event: QR Generated (`qr_generated`)
Fired when a user generates a new WiFi QR code.
*   `security_type`: `TEXT` ("WPA", "WEP", "none")
*   `is_hidden`: `INTEGER` (0 = No, 1 = Yes)

### Event: QR Scanned (`qr_scanned`)
Fired when a network code is successfully scanned.
