Share your WiFi network instantly and securely without typing passwords! WiFi QR Code Generator Sharer is a fast, 100% offline tool to create sharing codes and scan to join networks.
`
Generate high-quality QR codes for WPA, WPA2, WEP, and hidden networks on your device—no data is ever sent to external cloud servers.
`
CORE FEATURES:
• WiFi QR Generator: Input SSID and password to render custom vector codes.
• QR Scanner: Scan codes with your camera to join networks programmatically.
• Zoom Sharing: Pinch-to-zoom code display so friends can scan from distance.
• Local History: Store generated codes to retrieve and display later.
• 100% Private: All networks are saved locally in private sandbox settings.
