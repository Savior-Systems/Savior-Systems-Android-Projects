# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)
```
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: WiFi QR Sharer
*   **Category**: Tools
*   **Core Objective**: Build a fast, offline-first WiFi sharing utility utilizing QR codes. It targets search intent for: `wifi qr code`, `share wifi`, and `scan qr wifi`.
*   **Value Proposition**: Connect to networks instantly via scanning, generate sharing codes without revealing passwords, and save generated QRs offline.
```
---
```
## 2. Target Persona
### Persona: Julian (Tech-savvy Airbnb Host)
*   **Needs**: Quickly generate and print or display a WiFi QR code for guests to join his home network instantly.
*   **Pain Points**: Clunky QR creators that require uploading passwords to third-party web servers or spam heavy fullscreen ads when showing a code.
*   **How the App Solves it**: The application runs completely offline, generating QR vector bitmaps directly on the device.
```
---
```
## 3. Core Feature List
1.  **WiFi QR Generator**:
    *   Fields: SSID (Network Name), Password, Encryption Type (WPA/WEP/None), Hidden SSID flag.
    *   Generates crisp QR code standard specification (`WIFI:S:SSID;T:WPA;P:PASSWORD;;`).
2.  **QR Code Scanner**:
    *   Uses CameraX to scan WiFi QR codes and instantly configure/connect to the network.
3.  **Saved Codes History**:
    *   Local history of generated QR codes for rapid retrieval.
```
---
```
## 4. Monetization & Ad Zones
*   **Zero-Ad Zones**:
    *   The active camera scanner viewfinder and the full-screen QR display screen are kept 100% ad-free to prevent scanner delay and visual clutter during scans.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   Banner ad at the bottom of the input form.
    *   Interstitial ad on saving a network to history, governed by a **180s cooldown**.
```
