# 08. Play Policy & Data Safety — Smart Age & BD Date

Ensuring 100% compliance with Google Play Store Developer Policies regarding privacy, data collection, and system permissions.

---

## 1. System Permissions Audit

To maximize trust and safety, we strip all unnecessary permissions from the manifest.

### Required Permissions
*   `android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS` (SDK 33+): Required to fire birthday reminder alerts.
*   `android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` (SDK 31+): Required to trigger birthday alarms exactly at 09:00 AM local time.

### Stripped / Unused Permissions (🚫 Zero Tolerance)
*   🚫 `android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` (Not used - location is not tracked)
*   🚫 `android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` (Not used)
*   🚫 `android.permission.READ_CONTACTS` (Birthdays are keyed in manually, not imported)
*   🚫 `android.permission.INTERNET` (Requested only by AdMob / Firebase SDKs; the app logic itself uses zero network sockets)

---

## 2. Play Console Data Safety Declarations

Since this is a strictly offline-first application, the Data Safety form will be declared as follows:

| Section Question | Answer Status | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Does your app collect or share user data?** | **No** | All calculations, birthday profiles, and logs remain locally inside the SQLite Room DB. |
| **Is all user data encrypted in transit?** | **N/A** | No data is transmitted off the device. |
| **Can users request their data be deleted?** | **Yes** | Users can wipe all local profiles and history instantly via the Settings Screen. |

---

## 3. Exact Alarm Justification

*   **Trigger**: Local alarm scheduling.
*   **Policy Rule**: Exact alarms must only be used for user-facing actions that require precise timing (e.g. timers, calendars, or health reminders).
*   **Justification**: User-created birthday countdown alarms must trigger exactly on the morning of the event to ensure timely well-wishes, satisfying the policy requirement.
