# 12. Logging & Analytics

This document details logging rules and analytics setup for **Exam Countdown BD**.

---

## 1. Privacy Mandate
*   **Zero PII**: Exam names, custom milestones, and calendar dates must **never** be logged to analytics dashboards.

---

## 2. Analytics Events

### Event: Exam Countdown Added (`countdown_created`)
Fired when a user schedules a new target exam date.
*   `category_type`: `TEXT` (SSC, HSC, BCS, Admission, Custom)
*   `days_range`: `INTEGER` (Days until exam, e.g. 90)
