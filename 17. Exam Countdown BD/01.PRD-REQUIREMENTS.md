# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)
```
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: Exam Countdown BD
*   **Category**: Education / Utilities
*   **Core Objective**: Build a fast, offline-first exam countdown tracker tailored for students in Bangladesh. It targets search intent for: `exam tracker`, `hsc countdown`, and `bcs exam prep`.
*   **Value Proposition**: Precise second-by-second countdown timelines, local push alerts, custom academic categories, and 100% offline security.
```
---
```
## 2. Target Persona
### Persona: Fahim (BCS Aspirant in Dhaka)
*   **Needs**: Track multiple exam timelines simultaneously (e.g. BCS Preliminary, varsity admission dates, bank jobs) and stay reminded of final weeks.
*   **Pain Points**: Countdown tools that force online sign-in, run heavy background processes, drain batteries, or crash on startup.
*   **How the App Solves it**: Fahim inserts his target exam dates, which are stored locally in Room. The app renders clean, precise countdown panels instantly.
```
---
```
## 3. Core Feature List
1.  **Multiple Exam Dashboard**:
    *   Dynamic listing cards showing remaining Days, Hours, Minutes, and Seconds.
2.  **Milestone Task Checklist**:
    *   Sub-todos inside each exam card (e.g. "Complete Math Chapter 5", "Solve 2024 Past Papers").
3.  **Local Reminders**:
    *   System alarm notifications scheduled at 30 days, 7 days, and 1 day remaining marks.
4.  **Local Data System**:
    *   Category chips (HSC, SSC, BCS, Admission).
```
---
```
## 4. Monetization & Ad Zones
*   **Zero-Ad Zones**:
    *   Active countdown visual screens and milestone trackers are strictly zero-ad to keep students focused.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   Anchored adaptive banner at the bottom of the dashboard list.
    *   Interstitial ad on deleting a timeline or exiting Settings, governed by a **180s cooldown**.
```
