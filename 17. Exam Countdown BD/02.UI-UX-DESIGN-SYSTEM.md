# 02. UI/UX Design System

This document outlines structural visual layouts for **Exam Countdown BD**.

---

## 1. Color Palette
The primary style uses **Crimson Red** (`#C62828`) to invoke urgency, focus, and calendar deadlines.

### Dark Theme (Default)
*   **Background**: `#121212` / `#1E1414` (Deep Crimson Charcoal)
*   **Primary (Brand)**: `#C62828` (Crimson Red)
*   **Secondary (Brand Accent)**: `#FF8A80` (Coral/Rose)
*   **On-Background (Text Primary)**: `#FFEBEB` (Soft Red White)
*   **On-Background (Text Secondary)**: `#EF9A9A` (Muted Rose)

### Light Theme
*   **Background**: `#FAFAFA`
*   **Primary (Brand)**: `#D32F2F`
*   **Secondary (Brand Accent)**: `#FF5252`
*   **On-Background (Text Primary)**: `#3E1515`
*   **On-Background (Text Secondary)**: `#E57373`

---

## 2. Typography
*   **Display Title**: `Outfit Bold`, 26sp
*   **Countdown Numbers**: `Outfit Bold`, 28sp (Large numbers showing days/hours remaining)
*   **Headers & Cards**: `Inter Medium`, 16sp

---

## 3. Cards & Borders
*   **Timeline Cards**: Flat styling with a soft border utilizing the accent color (`#FF8A80` on dark) to highlight cards with less than 7 days remaining.
