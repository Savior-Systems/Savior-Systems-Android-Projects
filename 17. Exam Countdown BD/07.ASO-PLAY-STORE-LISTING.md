### Long Description (Max 4000 chars)
```
Stay ahead of your exam schedules and manage study plans effectively with Exam Countdown BD. A dedicated, offline-first exam tracker with countdown clocks designed for students in Bangladesh.

Monitor remaining Days, Hours, Minutes, and Seconds for important academic milestones instantly—without requiring internet connectivity or account creation.

CORE FEATURES:
• Multi-Exam Dashboard: Display countdown cards for all upcoming tests.
• Custom Academic Categories: Mapped for BCS Preliminary, HSC, SSC, University Admissions (DU, BUET, Medical), and local job prep.
• Milestone Checklists: Add micro-tasks inside exam cards to check off study progress.
• Local Push Alerts: Receive notifications at key milestones (30 days, 7 days, 1 day left).
• Zero-Ad Study Zone: Keep active study checklists completely distraction-free.

Practice smart preparation offline. Download today!
```
