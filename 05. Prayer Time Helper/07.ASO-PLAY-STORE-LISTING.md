### 1.2 Full Description (Excerpt)
*Never miss a prayer, wherever you are.* 
`
Prayer Time Helper is a privacy-focused, fully offline Islamic app designed to give you precise Namaz timings, reliable Azan alarms, and an accurate Qibla direction compass without tracking your background location or draining your battery. 
`
**Key Features:**
🕌 **100% Offline Accuracy:** Uses mathematical astronomy to calculate exact timings for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha without needing the internet.
🕋 **Qibla Compass:** Instantly find the direction to Mecca with our smooth, sensor-driven compass. Works flawlessly while traveling.
⏰ **Custom Azan Alarms:** Set silent alerts, gentle beeps, or full Azan notifications for each individual prayer.
🌍 **South Asia Optimized:** Built-in support for University of Islamic Sciences (Karachi) calculation method and Hanafi Asr timings, perfect for Bangladesh, Pakistan, and India. 
📿 **Tasbih Counter:** A simple, distraction-free digital dhikr counter with haptic feedback.
🔒 **Privacy First:** We respect your data. The app only needs your location once during setup, and you can even type your city manually to avoid GPS permissions entirely.
