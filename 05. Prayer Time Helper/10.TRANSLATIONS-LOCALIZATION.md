## 3. RTL (Right-to-Left) Support Rules
*   For Urdu and Arabic, the UI must flip horizontally.
*   In `AndroidManifest.xml`, ensure `android:supportsRtl="true"`.
*   Use `Start` and `End` padding/margins instead of `Left` and `Right` in all Compose Modifiers.
*   The Qibla compass dial itself does **NOT** flip. North is always up. Only the text overlaying the compass changes.
