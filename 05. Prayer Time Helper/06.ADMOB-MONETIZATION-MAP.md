# 06. AdMob Monetization Map — Prayer Time Helper

Religious apps demand high sensitivity regarding advertisement placement. Aggressive or inappropriate ads during spiritual moments (like prayer time countdowns or inside the Qibla compass) result in immediate uninstalls and 1-star reviews.

This monetization strategy prioritizes user respect and long-term retention over short-term aggressive revenue.

---

## 1. Ad Format Strategy

### 1.1 Adaptive Banner Ads (Primary Revenue)
*   **Placement**: Pinned to the absolute bottom of the Main Dashboard (below the Navigation Bar, or directly above it depending on layout).
*   **Behavior**: Auto-refreshes every 30–45 seconds.
*   **UX Safety**: Does NOT float over content. Has a distinct background color to separate it from the prayer timings list.

### 1.2 Interstitial Ads (Secondary Revenue)
*   **Frequency Cap**: STRICT 1 impression per 4 hours per user.
*   **Trigger Point**: Triggers ONLY when exiting the Settings screen or exiting the Tasbih counter back to the Dashboard.
*   **Absolute Restriction**: NEVER trigger an interstitial upon app open, during a prayer countdown (last 5 minutes), or while the Qibla compass is active.

### 1.3 Native Advanced Ads (Optional / Phase 2)
*   **Placement**: Embedded inside the "Dashboard" scroll view, below the final prayer (Isha) and above the footer.
*   **Styling**: Matches the Emerald/Midnight color scheme seamlessly. Requires `TemplateView` customization.

---

## 2. strict Ad Content Filtering (Play Console / AdMob Settings)

To maintain trust with the Muslim demographic, specific ad categories **MUST** be blocked at the AdMob account level for this specific Ad Unit.

### Required Blocks in AdMob:
1.  **Sensitive Categories**: Block Dating, Gambling, Alcohol, Adult content, Weight loss/Cosmetic procedures, and Religion (to prevent competing sects or controversial religious ads).
2.  **Review Center**: Regularly monitor the Ad Review Center to block specific creatives showing immodest clothing or culturally inappropriate imagery.

---

## 3. Placement Exclusions (Zero-Ad Zones)

Ads are completely disabled on the following screens to preserve the spiritual utility:
*   **Qibla Compass Screen**: No banners, no interstitials. The compass requires absolute focus and screen real estate.
*   **Active Tasbih Screen**: No banners during active Dhikr counting to prevent accidental clicks while tapping.
*   **Location Setup / Onboarding**: No ads during critical privacy permission flows.

    }
}
