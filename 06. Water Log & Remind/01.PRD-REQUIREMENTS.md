# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD) — Water Log & Remind
`
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: Water Log & Remind
*   **Package**: `com.saviorsystems.waterlogremind`
*   **Category**: Health & Fitness
*   **Core Objective**: Build a visually engaging, completely offline hydration tracker with smart reminders and one-tap home screen widgets, targeting Tier-1 markets (US, UK, CA, AU).
*   **Privacy Promise**: Zero cloud syncing of health data. Everything is stored locally. No registration required.
*   **Target Keywords**: `drink water reminder`, `hydration tracker`, `water logging app`, `daily water intake`, `water reminder widget`
`
---
`
## 2. Target Persona
`
### 2.1 Primary — The Health-Conscious Professional (Tier-1 Market)
*   **Demographics**: Age 22–45, office worker or remote employee.
*   **Pain Points**: Forgets to drink water due to busy schedules. Hates apps that require creating an account or logging in just to track water. Dislikes opening the app for every single glass of water.
*   **Goal**: Wants a seamless way to log water intake (preferably from a home screen widget) and get polite, customizable reminders.
`
---
`
## 3. Core Feature List
`
### 3.1 Smart Hydration Goal Calculator
*   **Inputs**: User's weight, gender, and general activity level (Sedentary, Active, Athlete).
*   **Output**: A calculated daily water goal in ml or oz.
*   **Weather Integration (Optional/Manual)**: A toggle for "Hot Climate" which adds a +15% modifier to the daily goal.
`
### 3.2 Liquid Visualization Dashboard
*   **Dynamic UI**: The main screen features a large, animated "water container" (circle or humanoid silhouette) that visually fills up as the user logs drinks.
*   **Progress Indicators**: Clear display of current intake vs. goal, and a percentage counter.
`
### 3.3 Frictionless Logging (In-App & Widgets)
*   **Quick Add Buttons**: Preset buttons (e.g., 200ml glass, 500ml bottle) for 1-tap logging.
*   **Custom Beverages**: Ability to log drinks other than water (Coffee, Tea, Juice) which apply a hydration multiplier (e.g., Coffee = 0.8x hydration).
*   **Jetpack Glance Widget**: A home screen widget that shows the current progress bar and includes 1-tap quick add buttons, bypassing the need to open the app.
`
### 3.4 Smart Reminder Engine
*   **Interval Timers**: Reminders every X minutes/hours.
*   **Quiet Hours**: "Do Not Disturb" periods (e.g., 10 PM to 7 AM) where no alarms fire.
*   **Contextual Nudges**: Notification text variations (e.g., "Time for a sip! 💧", "You're 50% there, keep hydrating! 🌊").
`
### 3.5 History & Analytics
*   **Calendar View**: A visual calendar showing days where the goal was met (green dots) vs missed (red dots).
*   **Charts**: Weekly and monthly bar charts showing average intake.
*   **Streaks**: A gamification counter showing the current consecutive streak of hitting the daily goal.
`

## 4. User Stories
`
| ID | Story | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| US-01 | As a user, I want the app to calculate a custom daily goal based on my weight so I know exactly how much to drink. | P0 |
| US-02 | As a busy user, I want a home screen widget with quick-add buttons so I can log a glass of water without opening the app. | P0 |
| US-03 | As a user, I want to set a "Wake Up" and "Bed Time" so I don't get notifications while sleeping. | P0 |
| US-04 | As a user, I want the main screen to visually fill up with water to give me a sense of accomplishment. | P0 |
| US-05 | As a user, I want to log coffee or tea, not just plain water, and have it calculate properly. | P1 |
| US-06 | As a user, I want to see a calendar of my past hydration history to track my consistency. | P1 |
| US-07 | As a user, I want to switch between Milliliters (ml) and Ounces (oz) depending on my region. | P1 |
`
