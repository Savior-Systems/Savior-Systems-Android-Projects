# 08. Play Store Policy & Safety — Water Log & Remind
`
Health and tracking apps must adhere to strict Google Play policies regarding User Data and Health App declarations. This document outlines our compliance strategy.
`
---
`
## 1. Permissions Audit (`AndroidManifest.xml`)
`
### Required Permissions (Justified)
*   `INTERNET`: Required ONLY for serving AdMob ads and Firebase Analytics telemetry.
*   `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`: Required by AdMob.
*   `VIBRATE`: Required for haptic feedback when tapping the Quick-Add widget or in-app buttons.
*   `POST_NOTIFICATIONS`: Required (Android 13+) to show the water reminder alerts.
*   `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`: Required (Android 12+) to guarantee reminders fire at precise intervals if the user requires strict schedules.
*   `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`: Required to reschedule AlarmManager reminders and the Midnight Reset WorkManager after a device reboot.
`
### STRIPPED Permissions (DO NOT ADD)
*   🚫 `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` / `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`: Not needed for a water tracker.
*   🚫 `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` / `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`: Not needed.
`
---
`
## 2. Health App Declaration Policy
`
Google Play has specific policies for apps that handle health or medical information.
`
**Our Defense & Strategy:**
1.  **Classification**: Water Log & Remind is a "Fitness & Wellness" app, NOT a medical device.
2.  **Disclaimer**: The app will include a standard disclaimer in the settings and onboarding: *"This app is for general wellness purposes only. It does not provide medical advice. Consult a physician for specific hydration needs."*
3.  **Data Isolation**: We do not sync weight, gender, or intake data to the cloud. All user profile data resides exclusively in local `DataStore` and `Room` databases.
`
---
`
## 3. Alarm & Exact Notification Compliance
`
Starting Android 14, `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` is heavily restricted.
**Our Defense:**
1.  A core feature of the app is "Reminders". Users rely on timely notifications to drink water.
2.  If challenged, we will state: *"This application serves as an active health reminder tool. Users configure specific interval alarms (e.g., every 60 minutes) to manage their hydration schedule. Inexact background batching defeats the core utility of the app."*
3.  **Fallback**: If `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` is denied, the app gracefully degrades to using `WorkManager` periodic requests (which may fire inexactly depending on OS Doze constraints).
`

`
## 4. Data Safety Form Responses
`
| Category | Data Type | Collected | Shared | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health & Fitness** | Fitness info (Intake) | Yes | No | App Functionality. Data remains on device. |
| **Personal Info** | Weight, Gender | Yes | No | App Functionality (Goal Calc). Data remains on device. |
| **Device IDs** | Device or other IDs | Yes | Yes (AdMob) | Advertising, Analytics (Firebase). |
`
*   **Is Data Encrypted in Transit?**: Yes (HTTPS for Ads/Analytics).
*   **Can Users Request Deletion?**: Yes (Clear App Data resets everything).
