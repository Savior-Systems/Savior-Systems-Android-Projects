# 07. ASO & Play Store Listing — Water Log & Remind

## 1. Store Metadata

### 1.1 Title & Subtitle
*   **App Title (30 chars)**: Water Tracker & Reminder
*   **Short Description (80 chars)**: Stay hydrated! Smart water reminders, widgets, and daily hydration tracking.

### 1.2 Full Description (Excerpt)
*Build healthy hydration habits effortlessly.*

Water Log & Remind is your personal hydration coach. Calculate your daily water goal, log drinks instantly from your home screen widget, and receive smart, gentle reminders throughout the day to keep your body healthy and energized.

**Key Features:**
💧 **Smart Daily Goal:** We calculate exactly how much water you need based on your weight, gender, and activity level.
⏱️ **Intelligent Reminders:** Get notified at optimal intervals. Set your wake-up and sleep times so we never disturb your rest.
📱 **Home Screen Widgets:** Log a glass of water in one tap without even opening the app.
☕ **Track All Beverages:** Not just water! Log coffee, tea, juice, and more with customized hydration multipliers.
📊 **Streaks & Insights:** Watch your progress visually fill up, maintain daily streaks, and view detailed weekly/monthly hydration charts.
🔒 **100% Private & Offline:** No accounts, no logins, no cloud syncing. All your health data stays safely on your device.

Start your hydration journey today for better skin, more energy, and a healthier you!
