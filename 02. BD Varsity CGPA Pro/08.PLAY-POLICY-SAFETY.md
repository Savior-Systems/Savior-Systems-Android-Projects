# 08. Play Console Policy & Safety
`
This document defines the compliance, privacy, and policy configuration for **BD Varsity CGPA Pro**. All rules align with [PLAY-CONSOLE-POLICY-GUARDRAILS.md](../PLAY-CONSOLE-POLICY-GUARDRAILS.md).
`
---
`
## 1. Permissions Required
`
| Permission | Required? | Justification |
|:-----------|:----------|:-------------|
| Internet (`INTERNET`) | Yes (manifest only) | Required for AdMob ad loading. No runtime prompt needed. |
| Network State (`ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`) | Yes (manifest only) | Check connectivity before ad requests. No runtime prompt. |
| All other permissions | **No** | App operates 100% offline for core functionality. |
`
**Runtime Permissions**: **Zero**. This app does not request any runtime permissions from the user. Camera, storage, location, contacts — none are needed.
`

## 3. Privacy Policy
`
*   **URL**: `https://savior-systems.github.io/privacy-policies/bdcgpa.html`
*   **Scope**: Must clearly state:
    *   The app is offline-first and does not transmit personal academic data
    *   No student grades, course information, or CGPA results are sent to any server
    *   Non-personal device IDs may be processed by Google AdMob for ad serving
    *   Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage events (no PII)
    *   Firebase Crashlytics collects anonymous crash reports
    *   Users can request data deletion by contacting `support@saviorsystems.com`
`
### Privacy Policy Template Content
`
```
Privacy Policy for BD Varsity CGPA Pro
`
Last updated: [Date]
`
BD Varsity CGPA Pro ("the App") is developed by Savior Systems. This privacy 
policy explains how we handle your data.
`
DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT:
- Your grades, courses, or CGPA results
- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your university affiliation
- Your location
`
The App operates entirely offline. All academic data you enter (courses, grades, 
semesters) is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our 
servers or any third party.
`
DATA COLLECTED BY THIRD-PARTY SDKs:
- Google AdMob: Non-personal advertising IDs for serving relevant ads
- Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage metrics (screen views, feature usage)
- Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash and error reports
`
For users in the EEA/UK, a consent dialog (Google UMP) is shown before any 
advertising data is collected.
`
DATA DELETION:
You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Clear All Data" option 
in Settings, or by uninstalling the App.
`
CONTACT:
For privacy inquiries, contact: support@saviorsystems.com
```
