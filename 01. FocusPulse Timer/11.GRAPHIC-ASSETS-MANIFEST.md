# 11. Graphic Assets & Icon Manifest

This document outlines the required graphics, icons, and visual assets for FocusPulse Timer, ensuring compliance with Google Play Store guidelines and Savior Systems' "Dynamic Asset Generator" persona standards.

## 1. App Icon Specifications

*   **Concept**: A minimalist, high-contrast flat vector representation of a classic Pomodoro tomato crossed with a modern timer ring.
*   **Background Color**: Solid Red (`#FFE74C3C`).
*   **Foreground Color**: Crisp White (`#FFFFFF`) with subtle internal shadows (`#D35400`) to provide depth without violating flat design principles.
*   **Shape Rule**: The main icon vector must be perfectly centered and allow for adaptive masking (circle, squircle, or rounded square) as per Android 8.0+ adaptive icon guidelines.

### Asset Dimensions
| Asset Type | Resolution | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Play Store Icon | 512x512 px | 32-bit PNG | Google Play listing. |
| Adaptive Foreground | 108x108 dp | Vector Drawable (XML) | Android app launcher (foreground). |
| Adaptive Background | 108x108 dp | Vector Drawable / Color | Android app launcher (background). |
| Legacy Icon | 48x48 dp | PNG (mdpi to xxxhdpi) | Older Android devices. |

## 2. Feature Graphic

*   **Resolution**: 1024x500 px.
*   **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha).
*   **Concept**: A bold, split-color background (Red `#FFE74C3C` and Navy `#2C3E50`) with the FocusPulse timer ring prominently displayed in the center. The tagline "Master Your Time" should be in the `Outfit` font, bold, positioned either above or beside the timer. Avoid overcrowding with device mockups.

## 3. Store Listing Screenshots

Generate a minimum of 4 to a maximum of 8 screenshots for phone form factors.

*   **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (Portrait).
*   **Style**: Device frames (optional but recommended) wrapped around the app UI, placed on contrasting pastel or solid color backgrounds.
*   **Text Banners**: Include short, punchy captions at the top of each screenshot using the `Outfit` font.
    *   *Screenshot 1*: "Stay Focused with Pomodoro" (Shows Main Timer).
    *   *Screenshot 2*: "Customize Your Workflow" (Shows Settings).
    *   *Screenshot 3*: "Track Your Productivity" (Shows Stats).
    *   *Screenshot 4*: "Dark Mode Ready" (Shows UI in Dark Theme).

## 4. In-App Assets

All in-app icons (e.g., play, pause, reset, settings gear, statistics chart) must be sourced from **Material Symbols** (Rounded or Sharp) to maintain consistency with the Jetpack Compose Material 3 theme. Do not mix and match icon families.
