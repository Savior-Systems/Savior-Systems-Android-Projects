# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## 1. Vision & Strategy
FocusPulse Timer provides a clean, distraction-free environment for users seeking to study or work. The application follows the Pomodoro Technique:
*   Work Session: 25 minutes.
*   Short Break: 5 minutes.
*   Long Break: 15 minutes (triggered after 4 consecutive work cycles).

## 2. Core User Stories
*   **Start/Pause Timer**: As a user, I can tap to start or pause the active timer cleanly.
*   **Custom Sessions**: As a user, I can adjust durations for work sessions and breaks in settings.
*   **Focus Soundscapes**: As a user, I can toggle background soundscapes (Rain, White Noise, Forest) to mask ambient noise.
*   **Session Logs**: As a user, I can view my daily study logs in a simple history tab.

## 3. Scope of MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
*   Standard countdown timer interface.
*   Interactive Settings screen for custom intervals.
*   Persistent Room database to log completed work cycles.
*   AdMob banner and interstitial ads.
