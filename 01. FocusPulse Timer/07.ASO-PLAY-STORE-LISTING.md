## 2. English (Primary Listing)

**App Title** (30/30 chars):
```
FocusPulse: Study & ADHD Timer
```

**Short Description** (79/80 chars):
```
Pomodoro timer with streaks, sounds & focus cards. Built for ADHD & deep work.
```

**Full Description** (≈ 3,800 chars — keyword-rich, benefit-first structure):
```
🎯 BUILT FOR BRAINS THAT WON'T SLOW DOWN.

FocusPulse is the Pomodoro study timer designed for ADHD students, deep work
professionals, and exam preppers who need structure without frustration.

── WHY FOCUSPULSE? ──

Most timers are boring. FocusPulse turns every study session into a streak,
every completed session into a shareable Focus Card, and every week into a
Focus Challenge. It's not just a timer — it's your daily focus ritual.

── CORE FEATURES ──

🍅 POMODORO TIMER
Classic 25-min focus / 5-min break cycles with customizable intervals.
Adjust your work session from 5 to 120 minutes — your brain, your rules.

🔥 DAILY STREAK & SHIELDS
Build an unbreakable study habit. Earn a free Streak Shield every day to
protect your streak even when life gets in the way.

⚡ WEEKLY FOCUS CHALLENGE
Every Monday, a new challenge drops. Complete 10, 20, or 30 sessions this
week. Finish the challenge, earn rewards, unlock exclusive focus card themes.

📊 STUDY HISTORY & HEATMAP
See your focus patterns on a 30-day calendar heatmap. Discover your best days
("You focus best on Tuesdays ☀️") from real session data.

🃏 FOCUS CARD — SHARE YOUR WIN
After every session, FocusPulse generates a beautiful shareable card with your
total focus time and streak. Share to WhatsApp, Instagram Stories, or anywhere.

🎵 FOCUS SOUNDSCAPES
Study in silence, or block distractions with Rain, White Noise, Forest Sounds,
or Cafe Ambience. All sounds work offline — no streaming required.

📱 HOME SCREEN WIDGET
See your timer, session count, and streak right on your home screen. Start a
session with one tap — without even opening the app.

🌙 FULL DARK MODE + AMOLED
Three theme modes: Light, Dark, and true AMOLED Black for battery saving on
OLED displays.

── DESIGNED FOR ADHD ──

FocusPulse is built with ADHD brains in mind:
✅ Simple, distraction-free interface — no clutter, no subscriptions, no logins.
✅ Smart daily goal adaptation — the app learns your pace and adjusts automatically.
✅ Streak shields for imperfect days — because life happens.
✅ Weekly challenges for momentum — not punishment.

── 100% FREE. 100% OFFLINE. ──

No internet required. No account signup. No cloud sync. Your data stays on
your device forever. We collect zero personal data — FocusPulse just works.

── PERFECT FOR ──
• ADHD students & adults
• University exam prep
• Remote workers & freelancers
• Deep work practitioners (Cal Newport fans 👋)
• Developers, writers, designers — anyone who works in focused blocks

Start your streak today. 🔥
```
