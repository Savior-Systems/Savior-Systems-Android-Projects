# 07. ASO & Play Store Listing

FocusPulse Timer is targeted at students, professionals, and remote workers looking for a minimalist Pomodoro timer to enhance their productivity and focus.

## 1. App Metadata

### Title & Subtitle
*   **App Title** (max 30 chars): `FocusPulse: Pomodoro Timer`
*   **Short Description** (max 80 chars): `Boost productivity with a minimalist Pomodoro timer. Stay focused & track time!`

### Long Description
```text
Enhance your productivity, eliminate distractions, and achieve deep work with FocusPulse Timer—your ultimate Pomodoro and study companion. Designed with a clean, minimalist interface, FocusPulse helps you break your work into manageable intervals, separated by short breaks, so you can stay fresh and focused all day.

🚀 Key Features:
• Classic Pomodoro Technique: 25-minute focus sessions with 5-minute short breaks.
• Customizable Timers: Adjust focus, short break, and long break durations to fit your unique workflow.
• Insightful Statistics: Track your daily focus time, completed sessions, and productivity streaks.
• Premium Ambient Sounds: Unlock calming background sounds (like rain, forest, or white noise) to aid concentration.
• Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes for late-night study or work sessions.
• Ad-Supported & Free: Enjoy core features completely free, with minimal, non-intrusive ads.

Perfect for studying for exams, coding, reading, or managing ADHD, FocusPulse Timer is lightweight and works fully offline. Transform the way you work and build healthy productivity habits today.

Download FocusPulse Timer and make every minute count!
```

## 2. ASO Keyword Strategy

*   **Primary Keywords**: Pomodoro timer, focus timer, study timer, productivity tracker, time management.
*   **Secondary Keywords**: ADHD timer, deep work, 25 minute timer, minimalist pomodoro, habit tracker.
*   **Target Audience**: Students, remote workers, freelancers, ADHD adults.
