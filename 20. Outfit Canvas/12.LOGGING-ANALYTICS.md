# 12. Logging & Analytics

This document details logging rules and analytics setup for **Outfit Canvas**.

---

## 1. Privacy Mandate
*   **Zero PII**: Uploads of clothing pictures, color swatch choices, or styling configurations must **never** be logged to analytics dashboards.

---

## 2. Analytics Events

### Event: Wardrobe Item Logged (`closet_item_added`)
Fired when a user adds a clothing item.
*   `category_type`: `TEXT` (tops, bottoms, shoes, outer)
*   `has_color_tag`: `INTEGER` (0 = No, 1 = Yes)
