# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)
`
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: Outfit Canvas
*   **Category**: Lifestyle
*   **Core Objective**: Build a fast, offline visual wardrobe and outfit planner. It targets search intent for: `outfit planner`, `style diary`, and `clothing organizer`.
*   **Value Proposition**: Local image cataloging, color-coordination guides, calendar outfit planners, and watermark-free sharing.
`
---
`
## 2. Target Persona
### Persona: Sophia (Eco-conscious Fashion Enthusiast)
*   **Needs**: Catalog her physical wardrobe (shirts, pants, shoes) to plan weekly styles visually, avoiding buying duplicate items.
*   **Pain Points**: Wardrobe planners that require cloud accounts, sell data to fashion brands, or load extremely slowly when displaying thumbnails.
*   **How the App Solves it**: Sophia snaps pictures of her clothes, which are saved in the app's local directory sandbox. The database maps layout cards instantly.
`
---
`
## 3. Core Feature List
1.  **Digital Closet Catalog**:
    *   Snaps or uploads clothing thumbnails.
    *   Tags: Category (Top, Bottom, Outerwear, Shoes), Color tag.
2.  **Visual Styling Canvas**:
    *   Drag and coordinate top/bottom pairs on screen.
    *   Color wheel guide to check matching harmony (analogous, complementary).
3.  **Style Calendar Diary**:
    *   Assign styled outfit combos to calendar dates.
`
---
`
## 4. Monetization & Ad Zones
*   **Zero-Ad Zones**:
    *   The active Outfit Drag-and-Match Canvas and color swatch matching screens are strictly ad-free to keep the user's styling experience smooth.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   Banner ad at the bottom of the Closet catalog screen.
    *   Interstitial ad on exporting/saving a combined styled collage layout, governed by a **180s cooldown**.
`
