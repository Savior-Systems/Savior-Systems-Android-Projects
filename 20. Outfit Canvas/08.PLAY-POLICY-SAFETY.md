# 08. Play Store Policy & Data Safety
`
This document outlines Google Play privacy policies for **Outfit Canvas**.
`
---
`
## 1. Requested Permissions
To maximize user trust and prevent automated app flags during review, the app requests the following permissions:
`
| Permission | API Level | Purpose | Necessity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `android.permission.CAMERA` | Any | Capture snapshots of physical clothing items. | **Required** for digital closet capture. Opt-in. |
| `android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` | API 33+ | Import clothing photos from device gallery. | **Required** for photo imports. Opt-in. |
`
No location, contact, phone, or network permissions are declared.
`
---
`
## 2. Google Play Data Safety Declarations
`
### A. Data Collection
*   **Data Collected**: None.
*   The application does not collect or transmit any user data (e.g., wardrobe photos, closet categories) to external servers. All files are saved locally.
`
### B. Data Sharing
*   **Data Shared**: None.
*   No personal data, device identifiers, or clothing photos are shared with third-party networks or service providers.
`
### C. Data Security Measures
*   **Encryption**: No data leaves the device.
*   **Data Deletion**: Users can delete their closet items by clearing app storage in system settings.
