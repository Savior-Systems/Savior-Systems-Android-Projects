# 02. UI/UX Design System
`
This document details the visual guidelines, typography, and styling components for **Fuel Mileage Log**.
`
---
`
## 1. Color Palette
The brand primary style uses **Emerald Green** (`#2E7D32`), giving the app an eco-friendly, fuel-efficiency, and wealth-saving aesthetic.
`
### Dark Theme (Default)
*   **Background**: `#121212` (Pure Dark) / `#1A231F` (Dark Forest surface)
*   **Primary (Brand)**: `#2E7D32` (Emerald Green)
*   **Secondary (Brand Accent)**: `#00C853` (Soft Lime/Mint)
*   **On-Background (Text Primary)**: `#E8F5E9` (Light Mint White)
*   **On-Background (Text Secondary)**: `#A5D6A7` (Muted Green Gray)
`
### Light Theme
*   **Background**: `#FAFAFA` (Off-white)
*   **Primary (Brand)**: `#2E7D32` (Emerald Green)
*   **Secondary (Brand Accent)**: `#00E676` (Mint Green)
*   **On-Background (Text Primary)**: `#1B5E20` (Forest Green Black)
*   **On-Background (Text Secondary)**: `#66BB6A` (Muted Leaf Green)
`
---
`
## 2. Typography
*   **Display Title**: `Outfit Bold`, 26sp
*   **Stats Numbers**: `Outfit Medium`, 24sp (For displaying MPG, L/100km)
*   **Body Copy**: `Inter Regular`, 14sp
*   **Label Titles**: `Inter Medium`, 12sp (Odometer, fuel price fields)
`
---
`
## 3. Cards & Gestures
*   **Dashboard Cards**: Rounded rectangles (radius `14dp`) with an elevated surface color, showing fuel economy stats.
*   **Swipe to Delete**: Swipe left on any mileage log entry in the log history list to instantly delete it.
`
