# 07. ASO & Play Store Listing

This document details target ASO search keywords, metadata titles, and listing descriptions for **Fuel Mileage Log**.

---

## 1. Keywords
*   **Primary**: `fuel log`, `gas mileage calculator`, `car mileage tracker`
*   **Secondary**: `fuel efficiency calculator`, `mpg log`, `odometer log`

---

## 2. Listing Metadata

### App Title (Max 30 chars)
`Fuel Mileage Log: Car Tracker`

### Short Description (Max 80 chars)
`Simple, offline fuel mileage log & efficiency tracker. Calculate MPG or L/100km.`

### Long Description (Max 4000 chars)
```
Keep track of your vehicle's fuel efficiency, costs, and mileage with Fuel Mileage Log. A 100% offline-first fuel tracker designed for speed and simplicity.

No account signups, no cloud syncing, and no internet required. Just open the app, enter your odometer reading, fuel volume, and purchase cost to instantly calculate your mileage metrics.

CORE FEATURES:
• Odometer Mileage Log: Track gas fill-ups and see your distance traveled.
• Fuel Economy Calculator: Automatic math supporting MPG (Miles per Gallon) and L/100km (Liters per 100 Kilometers) units.
• Cost Analytics: Monitor fuel price trends, average cost per unit distance, and total spend over time.
• 100% Private: All logs are stored securely in your phone's local database. We never upload your travel records.
• Data Export: Save your records anytime using local CSV spreadsheets.

Ideal for delivery drivers, road trippers, and daily commuters looking to manage gas budgets. Start tracking today!
```
