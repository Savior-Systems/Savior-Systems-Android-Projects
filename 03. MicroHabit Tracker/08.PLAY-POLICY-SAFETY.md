# 08. Play Store Policy & Safety — MicroHabit Tracker

This document details Savior Systems compliance rules, minimised permissions, and Google Play Data Safety requirements.

---

## 1. App Permission Scopes
To prevent Google Play policy warnings and maximize conversion rates, MicroHabit Tracker runs with **zero-permissions** required by default.

*   `android.permission.INTERNET` (Required for Firebase Analytics and AdMob banner loading).
*   `android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` (Required to reschedule daily local reminder alarms).
*   `android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` (Required to trigger local notifications precisely).

---

## 2. Google Play Data Safety Declarations

Our app collects minor telemetry for optimization. Here is the exact mapping to declare in your Play Console:

### 2.1 Data Collection Mapping
*   **Approximate Location:** Collected by AdMob SDK for localized ad delivery (Anonymous).
*   **User IDs (Optional):** Collected if user logs in to Cloud Sync (Encrypted in transit).
*   **App Performance Metrics:** Analytics metrics logged via Firebase Crashlytics (Anonymous).

### 2.2 Security Declarations
*   **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted from the app to Firebase Firestore is encrypted using HTTPS (SSL/TLS).
*   **Delete Mechanism:** Users can trigger a "Clear App Data" action in settings to delete all local database entities and preference files instantly.
