Start small, build big. MicroHabit Tracker helps you build long-term routines by focusing on tiny, 2-minute daily habits. Whether it's drinking a glass of water, doing 5 pushups, or reading a single page, MicroHabit ensures you stay consistent and maintain your daily streaks.

- Streak Protection: Earn "Freeze Days" to protect your progress on weekends or busy holidays.
- Offline-First: Zero network requirements. Your data stays private on your local storage.
- Interactive Home Widget: View and log your active habits directly from your home screen.
- Analytics Calendar: Visualize your streak milestones with color-graded calendar grids.
