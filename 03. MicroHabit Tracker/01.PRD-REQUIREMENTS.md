# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD) — MicroHabit Tracker
`
## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: MicroHabit Tracker
*   **Category**: Health & Fitness
*   **Core Objective**: Build a highly performant, offline-first streak-based habit tracking application focused on tiny daily habits (taking less than 2 minutes to perform) to build long-term routines.
`
---
`
## 2. Target Persona
*   **Busy Professionals & Students:** Users who want to build routines without spending time managing complex layouts.
*   **Goal Seekers:** Users motivated by streaks, visual calendar completions, and notifications.
`
---
`
## 3. Core Feature List
`
### 3.1 Main Dashboard
*   Direct view of active micro-habits on startup with zero loading delay.
*   Single-swipe checkoff gesture to log habit completion.
*   Compact, vertical timeline list highlighting today's accomplishments.
`
### 3.2 Streak Engine
*   Tracks current streak (consecutive days of completion) and longest streak.
*   Resilient local timezone calculation (UTC timestamp logged, local date matched).
*   *Habit Freeze:* Users can earn or toggle a "Freeze" day to protect their streak during weekends or holidays.
`
### 3.3 Historical Analytics
*   Minimal calendar grid overlay displaying color-graded completions.
*   Completion rate percentage cards.
`
### 3.4 Custom Home Widget
*   Jetpack Glance Home Widget containing today's habits with direct checkoff capabilities.
`
