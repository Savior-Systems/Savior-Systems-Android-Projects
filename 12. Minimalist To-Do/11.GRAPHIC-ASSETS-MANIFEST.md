# 11. Graphic Assets Manifest

This document details the visual assets checklist, dimensional specifications, and design guidelines for the Play Store deployment of **Minimalist To-Do**.

---

## 1. Visual Theme Summary
*   **Primary Accent Style**: Charcoal Gray (`#37474F`) and Soft Teal (`#4DB6AC`).
*   **Aesthetic Identity**: Clean checkmark glyph resting on an elegant Slate-Gray round-rect container. Free of glossy highlights, gradients, or complex shadows.

---

| Asset Type | Dimensions | Required Format | Location / Context | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Google Play Icon** | 512 x 512 px | 32-bit PNG (Max 1MB) | Play Console Store Listing | Stylized checkmark logo inside a rounded rectangle with Charcoal Gray background. |

---

## 3. Play Store Icon Design Details
*   **Icon File Name**: `minimalist_todo_icon.png`
*   **Foreground Design**: A clean, stylized white outline checkmark (`✓`) with a slight bottom shadow, combined with a Soft Teal accent dot in the top-right corner to draw focus.
*   **Background Design**: Solid Charcoal Gray `#37474F` flat container background.
*   **Mockup Visual**:
    *   Centered white checkmark glyph (70% opacity, 3px stroke width).
    *   No complex gradients or skeuomorphic grids.
