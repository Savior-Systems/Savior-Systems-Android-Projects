## 2. Play Store Listing Copy (English)
`
### App Title (Max 30 chars)
`Minimalist To-Do: Simple Tasks`
`
### Short Description (Max 80 chars)
`A simple, fast, and private offline to-do list and daily checklist manager.`
`
### Long Description (Max 4000 chars)
```
Looking for a clean, simple, and private way to organize your daily tasks? Minimalist To-Do is a zero-clutter, offline-first task manager designed to keep you focused.
`
No logins, no internet required, and no complex settings. Just a beautiful, minimalist workspace that opens instantly so you can capture tasks in under 5 seconds.
`
WHY USE MINIMALIST TO-DO?
• True Minimalism: No complex task parameters, tags, or syncing steps.
• Offline-First & Private: All your data is saved securely in your device's local database. Your thoughts and tasks stay 100% private to you.
• Gesture-Driven: Simply swipe right to complete a task, and swipe left to delete it. Clean and natural.
• Quick-Add Anchor: Instantly log tasks from the bottom input bar as soon as the app opens.
• Smart Custom Categories: Create groups like "Work", "Personal", "Groceries", or "Habits" to organize your workflow.
• Dark & Light Modes: Features a premium Charcoal Gray design system that is easy on the eyes.
`
PERFECT FOR:
- Daily task checklist
- Simple shopping lists
- Distraction-free reminders
- Quick brain dumps and thoughts
`
Get organized today with Minimalist To-Do. Keep it simple, keep it offline, and get things done!
```
