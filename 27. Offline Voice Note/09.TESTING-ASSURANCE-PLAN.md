# 09. Testing Assurance Plan

## 1. Developer Checklist Before Release

### Flow 1: Foreground Service & Microphone
- [ ] Tap record.
- [ ] Minimize the app. Lock the phone.
- [ ] Wait 2 minutes. Unlock phone, open app, stop recording.
- [ ] Playback the audio. **Verify:** Does it contain the full 2 minutes of audio? (If it stopped early, your Foreground Service is failing Doze Mode).

### Flow 2: Vertex AI Firebase Integration
- [ ] Record a 10-second clip saying "My name is John and I need to buy apples and milk."
- [ ] Tap "Generate AI Summary".
- [ ] **Verify:** Does the Firebase SDK successfully upload the audio chunk and return the transcript containing "John", "apples", and "milk"?

### Flow 3: Storage 
- [ ] Ensure recordings are saved in `Context.filesDir` (Internal Storage). Do not use `MediaStore` unless you specifically want the user's music player to find the voice notes.
