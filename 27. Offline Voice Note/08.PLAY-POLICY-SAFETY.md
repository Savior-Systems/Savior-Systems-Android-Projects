# 08. Play Policy & Safety

## 1. Critical Permissions Checklist
To pass Google Play Review, you must adhere to these policies:

- **`RECORD_AUDIO`:** Must display a prominent disclosure BEFORE requesting. "This app needs microphone access to record your voice notes."
- **Foreground Service Type:** In Android 14+, you MUST declare the foreground service type in `AndroidManifest.xml`.
  ```xml
  <service android:name=".VoiceRecorderService" android:foregroundServiceType="microphone" />
  ```
  You will need to justify this in the Play Console App Content section. The justification is simple: "App is a voice recorder that must continue recording when the screen is locked."

## 2. Data Privacy
- **Vertex AI Notice:** If the user clicks "AI Summary", their audio file is temporarily sent to Google's Vertex AI. You MUST mention this in your Privacy Policy. State clearly: "Audio is only transmitted if you explicitly request an AI summary. Otherwise, all recordings are kept 100% locally on your device."
