# 06. ADMOB MONETIZATION MAP

## 1. Ad Strategy
Voice apps are utilities. Users will uninstall if a banner ad blocks the record button.

## 2. Placements
1. **Native Advanced Ads:**
   - **Location:** Inside the `DashboardScreen` list (e.g., every 5th item).
   - **Why:** Blends in perfectly with the list of recordings.

2. **Rewarded Video Ad (The AI Gate):**
   - **Location:** The "Generate AI Summary" button on the Playback screen.
   - **Trigger:** Tapping the button shows a Dialog: "This feature uses advanced AI. Watch a short video to generate a summary for free."
   - **Why:** Calling the Vertex AI Gemini API costs money (Google Cloud billing). The eCPM from Rewarded Ads heavily offsets this cost, making the app highly profitable.

3. **Banner Ads:**
   - Place a standard banner ONLY at the very top of the `PlaybackScreen`. Do NOT place it on the `RecordingScreen` to avoid accidental clicks while trying to stop a recording.
