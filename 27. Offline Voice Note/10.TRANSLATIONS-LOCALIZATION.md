# 10. Translations & Localization

## 1. Strings Map
Use `strings.xml` for all text. Voice recorders are highly universal apps, making them perfect targets for French, Spanish, and Arabic localization in future updates.

```xml
<resources>
    <string name="app_name">Voice Note AI</string>
    <string name="action_record">Record</string>
    <string name="action_stop">Stop</string>
    <string name="action_ai_summary">Generate AI Summary</string>
    
    <string name="permission_mic_rationale">We need microphone access to record your voice memos.</string>
    <string name="ai_disclaimer">Generating an AI summary will temporarily process your audio in the cloud. Watch an ad to proceed.</string>
</resources>
```
