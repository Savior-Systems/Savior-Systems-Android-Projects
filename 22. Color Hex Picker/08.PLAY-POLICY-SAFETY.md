# 08. Play Console Policy & Safety Guardrails

## Critical Compliance Checks
1. **Data Safety Form**: Declare that ZERO user data is collected or shared off-device. The app is 100% offline.
2. **Permissions**: Do not request any dangerous permissions unless explicitly required by the core function (e.g., Camera for scanning).
3. **Ads Policy**: Ensure interstitials are easily dismissible and never appear unexpectedly (e.g., while the app is in the background or immediately on launch before the UI loads).

## UMP Compliance
AdMob UMP SDK is mandatory. Ads will only initialize after user consent is verified.
