# 08. Play Store Policy & Data Safety

This document outlines Google Play privacy policies for **Breathing Pacer**.

---

## 1. Requested Permissions
To maximize user trust and prevent automated app flags during review, the app requests **zero permissions**.

No location, contact, phone, storage, or hardware access permissions are declared.

---

## 2. Google Play Data Safety Declarations

### A. Data Collection
*   **Data Collected**: None.
*   The application does not collect or transmit any user data (e.g., custom timers, haptic preferences) to external servers.

### B. Data Sharing
*   **Data Shared**: None.
*   No personal data, device identifiers, or usage logs are shared with third-party networks or service providers.

### C. Data Security Measures
*   **Encryption**: No data ever leaves the device.
*   **Data Deletion**: Users can delete their customized settings by clearing app storage in system settings.
