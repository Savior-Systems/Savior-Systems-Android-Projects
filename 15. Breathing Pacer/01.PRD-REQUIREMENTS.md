# 01. Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## 1. Vision & Strategy
*   **App Name**: Breathing Pacer
*   **Category**: Health & Fitness
*   **Core Objective**: Build a guided breathing visualization utility app for stress relief. It targets search intent for: `breathing exercise`, `box breathing`, and `anxiety relief pacer`.
*   **Value Proposition**: Zero logins, elegant visual cues, customizable cycle timers, and immersive breathing paces.

---

## 2. User Personas
### Persona: Emma (Busy Office Worker)
*   **Needs**: A clean tool to run quick, 2-minute box-breathing exercises during highly stressful work breaks.
*   **Pain Points**: Breathing apps that require premium subscriptions, show long video ads before starting, or force push notification permissions.
*   **How the App Solves it**: Emma opens the app, presses "Start", and immediately follows the smooth teal expanding circle (Inhale, Hold, Exhale, Hold) with zero ad interruptions.

---

## 3. Core Feature List
1.  **Breathing Visualizer Screen**:
    *   Dynamic canvas circle that expands and contracts smoothly.
    *   Instructional text overlays (e.g. "Inhale", "Hold", "Exhale").
2.  **Breathing Cycle Presets**:
    *   Box Breathing (4s In, 4s Hold, 4s Out, 4s Hold).
    *   Relaxation (4s In, 7s Hold, 8s Out).
    *   Custom Cycle Adjuster (adjustable inhale/exhale sliders).
3.  **Haptic/Audio Guidance**:
    *   Gentle vibrations at cycle boundaries (can be toggled off).
4.  **Local Settings**:
    *   Configure sound alerts, keep screen on toggle, and exercise durations.

---

## 4. Monetization & Ad Zones
*   **Zero-Ad Zones**:
    *   The active breathing visualizer screen and pacer canvas are 100% ad-free to keep the user focused and relaxed.
*   **Monetization**:
    *   Adaptive banner anchored at the bottom of the main configuration screen.
    *   Interstitial ad triggers upon finishing a breathing session or leaving Settings, governed by the **180s cooldown**.
