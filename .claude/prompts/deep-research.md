# Prompt: Deep Research for a New App

Use this prompt to conduct thorough research before generating the 14-file blueprint for any new app.

---

## Prompt

```
Act as a Senior Mobile Product Researcher.

I am about to generate the 14-file documentation blueprint for [APP_NAME], an Android utility app in the [CATEGORY] category.

Before I start writing, I need you to research the following:

1. **Competitive Landscape**: Search for the top 5 similar apps on Google Play. Identify:
   - Their core features.
   - Their weaknesses (from 1-star reviews).
   - Gaps we can exploit (features they don't have).

2. **Architecture Research**: What is the best technical approach for this app's core function?
   - Does it require any specialized libraries (e.g., Adhan for prayer times, iText for PDFs)?
   - Does it need any sensors (compass, accelerometer)?
   - Does it need background scheduling (AlarmManager, WorkManager)?
   - Can it be 100% offline?

3. **ASO Keyword Research**: What are the highest-volume, lowest-competition keywords for this app's category?
   - Identify 5 primary keywords and 5 long-tail keywords.
   - Identify the target market (Tier-1 English? South Asian? Global?).

4. **Play Store Policy Risks**: Are there any specific policies that could affect this app?
   - Does the category (health, finance, religion) trigger special policy reviews?
   - Are there any dangerous permissions that might be needed?

5. **Unique Selling Proposition (USP)**: Based on the research above, what is the single most compelling differentiator we should build this app around?

Output a structured research brief that I can use as the foundation for the 14 blueprint files.
```
